We are a team of experts dedicated to ensuring that you have access to clean, safe drinking water. Our mission is to help you identify potential issues with your water quality so that you can take the necessary steps to protect your health and wellbeing. Toyam is a research based website wherein you can check the water quality using the different parameters of the water . With the help of the research the website will comment about the quality of water. The website can be used as a prototype for the large project such as to build the model using real the real sensors and sensing the parameters physically and then with the help of the website getting the appropriate results.
